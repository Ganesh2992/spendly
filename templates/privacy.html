Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026

Your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what information Spendly collects when you use our personal expense tracking service, how that information is used, and the choices you have. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Data We Collect

To provide Spendly's core service, we collect the information you give us when you create an account and log expenses. This typically includes:

  • Account information such as your name, email address, and password (stored securely as a hash).
  • Expense records you choose to enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.
  • Basic session data needed to keep you signed in across requests.

We do not collect sensitive financial information such as bank account numbers, card details, or transaction histories from external accounts. Spendly works with the data you provide directly.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information you provide only to operate and improve Spendly. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • Store, retrieve, and display the expenses you log.
  • Generate summaries, category breakdowns, and time-period reports you request.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored against your account and is only accessible to you when you are signed in. We take reasonable steps to protect your information using standard security practices, including password hashing, session management, and access controls.

While no system can be completely secure, we work to keep your data protected. You are responsible for keeping your account password confidential and for the accuracy of the expense data you record.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly relies on a small number of third-party services to operate, such as a web framework and a database. These services receive only the data needed to perform their function, and are expected to handle it in line with this policy and applicable laws.

Spendly does not integrate with advertising networks, analytics providers that build advertising profiles, or social media tracking pixels.

5. Your Choices

You can review, update, or delete the expense data you have recorded at any time from within the Spendly application. You may also request the deletion of your account; doing so will remove your account information and any associated expense records from active use.

If you have questions about your data or would like to make a request, please reach out through the support channels listed in the Spendly application.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our service or in legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how Spendly handles your information.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please reach out through the support channels listed in the Spendly application.
